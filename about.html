Meet Our Founder

Abdelaziz Bouajina

🇲🇦 Marrakech, Morocco Transport & Tour Expert

"Morocco is not just my country — it is my passion. Every tour I lead is a piece of my heart that I share with travelers from around the world."

My name is Abdelaziz, and I have been guiding international travelers across Morocco for over 17 years. What started as a passion for sharing my beautiful country has grown into Beldia Tours — a professional travel agency dedicated to creating authentic, personalized Moroccan experiences.

Based in Marrakech, I specialize in private transport and multi-day excursions across Morocco. From the golden dunes of the Sahara to the snow-capped Atlas Mountains, from the ancient medinas of Fes and Marrakech to the Atlantic coast of Essaouira — I know every road, every hidden gem, and every authentic restaurant that will make your journey unforgettable.

Our Story

How Beldia Tours Was Born

1

2007 — The Beginning

I started working as a local transport provider in Marrakech, helping international tourists navigate Morocco safely and comfortably. My passion for sharing Morocco's beauty with the world grew with every journey.

2

2010-2020 — Building Trust

Over a decade of working with travelers from Spain, France, Italy, USA, UK and beyond. Built a reputation for reliability, authenticity, and genuine hospitality. Every client became a friend.

3

2025 — Beldia Tours is Born

With years of experience and hundreds of happy travelers, we officially launched Beldia Tours — a professional agency dedicated to private, personalized Morocco experiences with a modern online presence at beldiatours.com.

Today — Your Journey, Our Passion

Beldia Tours is rated Excellent on TripAdvisor with clients from Spain, France, Italy, India, Argentina and beyond. We continue to grow — one unforgettable journey at a time.
