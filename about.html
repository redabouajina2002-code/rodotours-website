Welcome to Beldia Tours

Authentic Morocco,
Your Way

"Morocco is not just my country — it is my passion. Every tour I lead is a piece of my heart that I share with travelers from around the world."

Based in Marrakech, we specialize in authentic and personalized tours across Morocco. From the golden dunes of the Sahara Desert to the Atlas Mountains, Chefchaouen, and the Imperial Cities — we create experiences that combine adventure, culture, and comfort.

Every itinerary is carefully planned to help you experience the real Morocco with knowledgeable local drivers, exceptional service, and a genuine passion for sharing the beauty of our country.

Our expert guide Abdelaziz has over 17 years of experience guiding international travelers from Spain, France, Italy, USA, India, Argentina and beyond — always with a smile, always with authenticity.
