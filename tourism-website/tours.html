3 Days / 2 Nights Max 12 Marrakech → Merzouga

Marrakech to Merzouga Desert Tour

Experience the ultimate Saharan adventure. Drive through the scenic Draa Valley, visit the UNESCO-listed Ait Ben Haddou kasbah, and arrive at the breathtaking Erg Chebbi dunes for a magical camel ride at sunset and a night under a billion stars.

🐪 Camel Trek 🌅 Sunrise Dunes ⭐ Desert Camp 🏛️ Ait Ben Haddou 🎵 Berber Music
📅 Day 1 – Marrakech → Ait Ben Haddou → Merzouga

Early morning departure from Marrakech heading south via the High Atlas Mountains and Tizi N'Tichka pass (2,260m). Visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Ait Ben Haddou kasbah. Continue through the Draa Valley to reach Merzouga village at the edge of the Sahara.

📅 Day 2 – Sahara Desert Camel Trek & Camp

Morning exploration of Merzouga village. Afternoon camel trek into the Erg Chebbi dunes. Watch the spectacular sunset from the top of the dunes. Overnight in a traditional Berber desert camp with dinner and traditional music around the campfire under an incredible starry sky.

📅 Day 3 – Sunrise & Return to Marrakech

Wake up before dawn to watch the magical sunrise over the golden dunes. Camel ride back to the hotel for breakfast. Visit the Erfoud fossil market and Todra Gorge en route. Return to Marrakech in the evening.

7 Days / 6 Nights Max 10 4 Imperial Cities

Imperial Cities Grand Tour

Journey through Morocco's four magnificent imperial cities – Marrakech, Fes, Meknes, and Rabat. Discover ancient medinas, royal palaces, intricate mosques, and vibrant souks filled with artisans and the scents of exotic spices.

🕌 4 Medinas 🎨 Souks 🏰 Royal Palaces 🍵 Tea Ceremony 🎭 Gnawa Music
📅 Days 1–2 – Marrakech: Jemaa el-Fna & Souks

Explore Jemaa el-Fna square, the Koutoubia Mosque, the Bahia Palace, Saadian Tombs, and the colorful souks of the ancient medina. Evening food tour at the famous night market.

📅 Days 3–4 – Fes: The Ancient Medina

Discover Fes el-Bali – the world's largest car-free urban area. Visit the Chouara Tanneries, Bou Inania Madrasa, Al-Qarawiyyin University (the world's oldest), and the vibrant souks of the old medina.

📅 Day 5 – Meknes & Volubilis Roman Ruins

Visit the imperial city of Meknes – Bab Mansour gate, Moulay Ismail Mausoleum, Heri es-Souani granaries. Excursion to Volubilis, Morocco's best-preserved Roman ruins (UNESCO site).

📅 Days 6–7 – Rabat: Morocco's Capital

Explore Rabat's Kasbah des Oudaias, Hassan Tower, Mohammed V Mausoleum, and the Chellah necropolis. Free afternoon in the vibrant Avenue Mohammed V before departure.

2 Days / 1 Night Max 8 High Atlas

Atlas Mountains Excursion

Escape the city and immerse yourself in the breathtaking High Atlas Mountains. Trek through traditional Berber villages, visit spectacular waterfalls, and enjoy panoramic views of Morocco's highest peaks including Jbel Toubkal (4,167m).

🥾 Trekking 🏘️ Berber Villages 💧 Ouzoud Waterfalls 🎒 Hiking 🏔️ Toubkal Views
📅 Day 1 – Marrakech to Imlil Valley

Drive from Marrakech through the Asni valley to Imlil (1,740m). Trek to Berber villages of Aroumd and Sidi Chamharouch. Traditional Berber lunch with local family. Overnight in mountain gîte or riad.

📅 Day 2 – Mountain Trek & Return

Morning trek to the Tacheddirt plateau for spectacular Atlas panoramas. Visit the Ourika Valley and Setti Fatma waterfalls. Return to Marrakech in the afternoon with unforgettable mountain memories.

Chefchaouen & Rif Mountains Tour

Discover Chefchaouen – Morocco's most photographed city with its iconic blue-painted streets. Explore the Rif Mountains, visit Tangier on the Mediterranean, and discover the charming coastal city of Asilah with its painted murals.

🔵 Blue Medina 📸 Photography 🏔️ Rif Mountains 🌊 Tangier 🎨 Asilah Murals
📅 Day 1 – Fes to Chefchaouen

Drive from Fes through the Middle Atlas to Chefchaouen. Check in to traditional riad. Afternoon free to explore the magical blue medina. Sunset views from the Spanish mosque.

📅 Day 2 – Chefchaouen Exploration

Full day exploring Chefchaouen's medina with guide. Visit Ras el Ma waterfall, the Kasbah museum, and artisan workshops. Photography walk through the most scenic blue alleyways.

📅 Days 3–4 – Tangier & Asilah

Drive to Tangier – visit the Kasbah, American Legation, and Cap Spartel (where Atlantic meets Mediterranean). Next day, visit the artistic coastal town of Asilah with its painted medina walls before returning to your starting point.
