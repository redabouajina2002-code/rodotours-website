❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

🕐 How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance, especially for peak season (Oct–Nov, Mar–May). For luxury or large group tours, 6–8 weeks is ideal to ensure availability.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, PayPal, and cash (Moroccan Dirham or Euros). A 20% deposit confirms your booking, with the balance due on arrival.

🔄 What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 7 days before departure. 50% refund for cancellations 3–7 days before. No refund within 72 hours. We offer flexible rebooking for most tours.

🌍 Do I need a visa for Morocco?

Citizens of most Western countries (EU, USA, UK, Canada, Australia) don't need a visa for Morocco (up to 90 days). Check your specific country's requirements before booking.

🤝 Are your tours private or group?

We offer both! Private tours give you maximum flexibility. Small group tours (max 10–12) offer a social experience at lower cost. We can customize any tour for your needs.

🗣️ What languages do your guides speak?

Our guides are fluent in English, Spanish, French, Arabic, and Berber (Tamazight). We match you with the guide best suited to your language preference.
