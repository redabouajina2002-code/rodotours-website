Tour Overview

Escape the hustle of Marrakech and discover the stunning Ourika Valley — just 1 hour away in the foothills of the High Atlas Mountains. This full-day excursion takes you through traditional Berber villages, lush green valleys, and beautiful mountain scenery.

Visit local herb and spice gardens, walk through authentic Berber markets, explore traditional homes, and enjoy a delicious Moroccan lunch at a local restaurant overlooking the river. Perfect for families, couples, and solo travelers.

Day Itinerary

8:30am — Pickup from Marrakech

Your guide picks you up from your hotel and drives south through the foothills of the Atlas Mountains.

Morning — Herb & Spice Garden

Visit a traditional Berber herb and spice garden. Learn about natural Moroccan medicines, argan oil, and traditional remedies used for centuries.

Midday — Berber Village Visit

Walk through authentic Berber villages, meet local families, and experience real Moroccan mountain life. Visit the local souk (market) and browse handmade crafts.

Lunch — Moroccan Restaurant

Enjoy a delicious traditional Moroccan lunch at a local restaurant overlooking the Ourika river. Tagine, couscous, fresh salads — all included in the price.

Afternoon — Waterfalls & Valley Walk

Walk along the Ourika river to the beautiful waterfalls. Enjoy the stunning green mountain scenery and fresh mountain air — a perfect contrast to the Marrakech medina.

5:00pm — Return to Marrakech

Drive back to Marrakech arriving by early evening. Drop-off at your hotel or riad.
