Tour Overview

"Experience the magic of the Sahara on Morocco's most iconic desert road trip — a journey you will never forget."

This fully private 3-day tour takes you from Marrakech deep into the heart of the Moroccan Sahara. Travel through the dramatic High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka pass, visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Aït Ben Haddou, explore the Draa Valley and Valley of Roses, and finally arrive at the spectacular Erg Chebbi dunes in Merzouga.

Mount your camel at sunset and ride into the golden dunes, then spend the night in a traditional Berber desert camp under a billion stars. Wake up before dawn for an unforgettable Sahara sunrise before returning to Marrakech on Day 3.

🏔️ Atlas Mountains 🏯 Aït Ben Haddou UNESCO 🌹 Valley of Roses 💧 Todra Gorge 🐪 Camel Trek ⭐ Desert Camp 🌅 Sahara Sunrise

Day-by-Day Itinerary

1
Marrakech → Ouarzazate → Zagora
High Atlas Mountains · Aït Ben Haddou · Draa Valley

Morning — Departure from Marrakech

Your guide picks you up from your hotel at 7:30am. Drive south through the stunning High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka pass (2,260m altitude). Stop for photos at the mountain viewpoints and enjoy the dramatic scenery.

Midday — Aït Ben Haddou

Visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Aït Ben Haddou — the ancient fortified village (ksar) that served as a filming location for Gladiator, Game of Thrones, Lawrence of Arabia and many more. Walk through the village and climb to the top for panoramic views. Lunch at a local restaurant.

Afternoon — Ouarzazate

Drive through Ouarzazate — known as the "Hollywood of Africa". Optional visit to the famous Atlas Film Studios. Continue through the stunning Draa Valley, lined with thousands of date palms and ancient kasbahs.

Evening — Arrival & Overnight

Arrive at your hotel in Zagora or M'Hamid. Enjoy a traditional Moroccan dinner (demi-pension). Rest for tomorrow's desert adventure.

Lunch Included Dinner Included Hotel Overnight
2
Zagora → Merzouga → Sahara Desert
Draa Valley · Erg Chebbi Dunes · Camel Trek · Desert Camp

Morning — Draa Valley & Tamegroute

After breakfast, continue through the spectacular Draa Valley — Morocco's longest valley. Visit Tamegroute, famous for its green pottery workshops and 17th-century library. Drive through the vast desert landscapes towards Merzouga.

Afternoon — Arrival at Erg Chebbi

Arrive at the spectacular Erg Chebbi dunes — some of the highest in Morocco reaching 150 meters. Check into your desert camp, freshen up, and prepare for the most magical moment of your trip.

Sunset — Camel Trek into the Sahara

Mount your camel and trek deep into the golden dunes as the sun sets over the Sahara. Arrive at your traditional Berber desert camp as the stars begin to appear. Enjoy a traditional Moroccan dinner around the campfire with live Berber music.

Night — Stargazing in the Sahara

With zero light pollution, the Sahara offers one of the world's best stargazing experiences. Sleep in your comfortable desert tent and prepare for an unforgettable sunrise.

Breakfast Included Dinner Included Desert Camp
3
Sahara → Todra Gorge → Marrakech
Sunrise · Todra Gorge · Atlas Mountains · Return

Dawn — Sahara Sunrise

Wake up early to witness the magical sunrise over the Sahara dunes — one of the most breathtaking experiences in Morocco. The dunes turn from deep purple to vivid orange as the sun rises. Camel trek back to camp for breakfast.

Morning — Todra Gorge

Drive north through spectacular desert landscapes. Stop at the magnificent Todra Gorge — dramatic limestone canyon walls rising 300 meters high with a crystal clear river at the base. Walk through this natural wonder.

Afternoon — Return to Marrakech

Drive back through the High Atlas Mountains and arrive in Marrakech by early evening. Drop-off at your hotel or riad. Your unforgettable Morocco desert adventure is complete.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does the tour start?
We pick you up from your hotel in Marrakech at 7:30am on Day 1. We recommend having breakfast before pickup or we can arrange an early breakfast at your hotel.
What type of accommodation is provided?
Night 1: Comfortable hotel or riad in Zagora or Ouarzazate (private room with private bathroom). Night 2: Traditional Berber desert camp in Merzouga — comfortable beds, blankets, shared bathroom facilities. All accommodation is included in the price.
Is the camel trek difficult?
No — the camel trek is very easy and suitable for everyone including children and elderly. The trek lasts about 45-60 minutes into the dunes. You can also walk alongside the camel if you prefer.
What should I pack for this tour?
Comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, sunglasses, hat, warm layer for desert nights (can get cold even in summer), camera, personal medications, small backpack, cash for optional purchases and tips.
What is the cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before departure. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a fee. Please contact us directly via WhatsApp to discuss any changes to your booking.
Is this tour suitable for children?
Absolutely! This tour is very family-friendly. Children love the camel trek, desert camp, and stargazing. Our guides are experienced with families and will adjust the pace accordingly.
What is the best time of year for this tour?
The best months are October to April when temperatures are comfortable. Summer (June-August) can be very hot in the desert (40°C+) but the tour still runs. We recommend spring and autumn for the best experience.
